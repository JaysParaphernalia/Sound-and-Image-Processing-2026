### Week 7 Task


We were asked to design a sound to match a sci-fi scene where the main characters arrive on the planet, using at least three oscillators.


This sketch is a short audio that mimics an alien enviornment and uses:

- Sine wave for a softer tone

- Sawtooth for a harsh synthetic sound

- Triangle for sharper mids
